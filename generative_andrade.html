Project Inspiration & Vision

This project was inspired by Op Artist Edna Andrade’s artwork, which I aimed to recreate using her shape rules and system while applying a separate source of inspiration. Given that Andrade’s work resembles interior rooms with windows, I decided to turn this perspective outward and use the facade of Harvard’s Memorial Hall as inspiration for the interior designs.
